Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Powerful under the hood, simple on the surface. Syncora handles the heavy lifting so your files are always safe.

📁
Auto Backup
Point Syncora at any folder and it handles the rest. New files are automatically detected and uploaded without you lifting a finger.
⚡
Real-Time Sync
File watcher monitors your folder continuously. The moment a file is saved, Syncora catches it and queues it for upload in milliseconds.
✈️
Telegram as Storage
Leverage Telegram's generous limits as your cloud backend. Files up to 2GB each, stored in your private channel, forever.
🔒
Secure & Private
Uses your own Telegram account and API keys. No third-party server touches your files. Your data stays yours — completely.
📊
Smart File Logs
Every upload is tracked with timestamp, file size, and status. Search and filter your backup history with ease. Full audit trail, always.
🗑️
Auto Delete (Optional)
Free up local disk space automatically. Enable auto-delete and Syncora removes local copies after confirming a successful upload.
